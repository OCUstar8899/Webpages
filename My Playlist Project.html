Alternative Facts

My Playlist Organization

My playlist is called Alternative Facts. That's because of not knowing what else to call it. But the name fits the content.

A lot of 21 Pilots' songs are rocky and about depression and how to handle it. That's why half of my playlist is just their songs.

They represent something that is worthwhile to move on from. Finding their setlist for The Icy Tour was easy. It took basic research.

120/8=15. This project is organized by 8 tables of 15 values each for 6 categories. There are 48 individual screenshots in the folder.

My playlist is also organized alphabetically by song name. Instead of by artist. That makes it more interesting than monotonous.

My playlist lasts for 7 hours and 36 minutes. It consists of 60 Twenty One Pilots' songs and 60 songs by other artists. It is various.

My playlist is balanced between happy and sad songs. I thought it should be, for an emotional balance. There is a lot of meaning behind it.

Reading lyrics and listening reveals a topic. By inductive reasoning. Working from a conclusion to a general principle. What I think they're about.
